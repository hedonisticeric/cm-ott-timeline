About the speaker

This month we’ve partnered with Women in Communications and Technology NCR for a reflective February session on “valuing moments of pause”. Complementing this month’s theme “moments,” digital veteran Kristina Mausser will lead us in a discussion of the era of mobile communication and its troubling effects on our creativity.

Join us on Friday, February 24 at the beautiful Andaz Hotel as we take a moment and reflect on how to ensure what we create has lasting value.

Kristina Mausser is the president of Kina’ole Inc. and a senior content strategy consultant. A veteran of the web industry, she has worked with some of today’s leading organizations including Sephora, Microsoft, PepsiCo, Saks Fifth Avenue, the Ford Foundation, and the Cannes-award winning digital agency, The Barbarian Group. Kris regularly speaks at conferences around the world and is excited to be returning to Europe this summer to present at the SOAP! content conference in Krakow, Poland.
