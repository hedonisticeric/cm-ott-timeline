About the speaker

Craig Fitzpatrick is child nerd, college dropout, and serial entrepreneur. A startup lifer, Craig’s career spans 7 software startups, one dot-com boom and bust, one near personal bankruptcy, and most recently, founder and CEO of PageCloud - something he believes can fix the Internet.

Craig has been leading product teams for 20 years, has raised financing for 3 startups, two of which he founded. He is a well respected product designer, and member of the startup community.

Local partners

PageCloud is a professional-grade website creation tool that runs in the cloud but still integrates seamlessly with your desktop.
